The Rich History of Dingras

A legacy of courage and unity.

Dingras played a significant role during the Spanish era and the Philippine Revolution. It stood as a center of faith and resistance — a town known for its people’s resilience and bravery.

The old church ruins of Dingras are among the most historic landmarks, symbolizing both faith and endurance through time.
