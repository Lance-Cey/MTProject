Tourist Attractions in Dingras

Explore nature, history, and heritage in one place.

Balay Dingras

A beautifully restored ancestral house that serves as a museum and cultural center. It showcases the rich history and craftsmanship of the people of Dingras.

Madongan Dam

A breathtaking spot surrounded by green hills and flowing water — perfect for swimming, picnics, and sightseeing.

Church of Dingras

Known for its massive brick ruins, this historical church stands as a symbol of faith, strength, and resilience through time.
